The Shoe Store was established in 2006 with one goal in mind: to help our customers find the best fit for a running shoe. It is our belief that running shoes should fit properly, be comfortable and make you feel fast.

You want spikes? We got it. You want a daily trainer? We got it. We promise we will never cut corners or compromise on quality. We are proud of our combined 75 years of running shoe experience. All of out fitters are life-long runners and provide coaching services on the side. They literally eat, sleep & drink running. With every step, you'll feel the difference that sets us apart from our competitors. Trust us, you'll come running back!

If you're not completely satisfied, we will refund your purchase and help you find a shoe to help you meet your goals.
